Last updated: June 2026

1. Agreement to Terms

By registering for and participating in the Asian Rover Challenge (ASRC) 2026, you agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions. If you do not agree with any part of these terms, you may not participate in the competition.

2. Eligibility

Participation is open to undergraduate and graduate university students. Teams must consist of 5 to 30 members from the same or multiple institutions. All participants must provide valid student identification upon request.

3. Registration & Fees

  • Registration must be completed through the official portal before the deadline.
  • Registration fees are non-refundable unless the event is canceled by the organizers.
  • The organizing committee reserves the right to reject any registration that violates the competition rules.

4. Competition Conduct

All teams must adhere to the highest standards of academic integrity and professional conduct. Plagiarism, sabotage, or failure to follow safety protocols will result in immediate disqualification.

5. Intellectual Property

Teams retain full intellectual property rights to their rover designs and software. However, by participating, teams grant ASRC and its partners the right to use photographs, videos, and general descriptions of their rovers for promotional and educational purposes.

The Asian Rover Challenge, MACE Kothamangalam, and Srishti are not liable for any injuries, property damage, or losses incurred during the competition. Teams are responsible for their own safety and insurance.

For questions regarding these terms, contact us at contact@roverchallenge.org.
